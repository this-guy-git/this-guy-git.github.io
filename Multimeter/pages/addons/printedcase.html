3D Printed Case

A 3D printed case to protect the breadboard and other pieces. The case is designed to be printed in two parts, the top and the bottom. The top part has holes for the components and the bottom part has a slot for the battery pack. The case can be printed in any color and can be customized with different designs or logos. Specs:
  • Material: PLA or ABS
  • Layer Height: 0.2mm
  • Infill: 15%
  • Print Time: Approximately 4 hours
  • Dimensions: 183mm x 140mm x 23mm
  • Weight: Approximately 104g
