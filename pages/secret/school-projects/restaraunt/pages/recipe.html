Freddy's Famous Fry Seasoning Recipe

Ever craving some delicious Freddy's Famous Fry Seasoning but haven't had a recent Freddy's run? Don't want to order some from our menu? Here's a simple guide on how to make some at home with a few simple ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 5 tsp salt
  • 5 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Store in an airtight container.
  3. Use on your favorite foods or delicious shoestring fries!
