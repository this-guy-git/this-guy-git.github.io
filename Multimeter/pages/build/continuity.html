Continuity Measurement

What Continuity Is?

Continuity checks if electricity can flow through a wire or component. If the circuit is complete, the multimeter will detect it.

How It’s Used

  • Tests if wires or connections are broken.
  • Checks switches, fuses, or other components to see if they conduct electricity.
  • Helps find open circuits before powering up a device.
